What do I do?
As a society, we currently face several problems:
We are running out of resources.
The work of each of us can be replaced by AI.
We are all increasingly concerned about our safety.
We are steadily losing our freedom.
We are gradually losing real human contact.
But...
I don’t know exactly how to solve these problems, but I do know the path. First, we need to explore these issues, then discuss them, and finally resolve them. We have the energy to do this.

In my opinion, blockchain, GAI, and telecommunications can solve all these problems and unite humanity, which will also open the way beyond Earth, directly into space.

To fulfill this vision, I write blogs, articles, create video content, and, above all, I talk. This is how I bring my vision of the world closer.

It might seem utopian and foolish to you, and you have every right to think so. As I say, it’s always about communication. If you have something to say, reach out; I’d be happy to talk with you too. It will help everyone.
